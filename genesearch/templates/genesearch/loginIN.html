About RareVarDB

RareVarDB is a database designed by GeneSpectrum Life Sciences to provide researchers, clinicians, and geneticists with easy access to rare genetic disease variants. This comprehensive platform serves as a one-stop resource, offering in-depth information and variant annotations for a wide range of rare diseases. RareVarDB encompasses a collection of rare genetic diseases, with information on over 700 rare genetic diseases, 1200 genes, and 50000 variants.

Key Features of RareVarDB:

  • Extensive Rare Disease Variant Collection
  • Disease and Variant Annotation
  • Flexible Search Options
  • Integration of Variant Annotations
